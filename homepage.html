About Kimoja

Kimoja is a people-first digital system designed to bring fairness, accountability, and clarity to daily labor-based work. Built around teamwork and shared responsibility, Kimoja ensures that every worker sees their effort reflected clearly and honestly.

From truck offloading to daily attendance, from earnings distribution to payment history, Kimoja transforms manual record-keeping into a secure, transparent, and modern experience.

Our Core Principles

  • Transparency – Every shilling earned is visible and traceable.
  • Fairness – Payment is shared strictly by participation.
  • Unity – Different groups, one system, one vision.
  • Dignity of Work – Physical labor deserves digital respect.

Our Work Groups

Kimoja operates through independent work groups. Each group manages its own members, trucks, attendance, and payments while remaining part of the larger Kimoja community.

How It Works

  1. Admins record trucks, tonnage, and total payment.
  2. Only workers who offload a truck are marked present.
  3. Earnings are automatically calculated and distributed fairly.
  4. Workers track daily earnings, payment history, and progress.

Built for Longevity

Kimoja is not a temporary solution. It is designed to grow with teams, adapt to different work environments, and preserve historical records for accountability and trust.

Whether managing one group or many, Kimoja scales without losing clarity.

Access Your Space

Select your group and log in to view your dashboard, earnings, and progress. Admins manage operations. Workers see only what belongs to them.
